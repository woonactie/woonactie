THE ROUTE FOR SUNDAY

It's almost time: Sunday 6 February we will take to the streets to make ourselves heard against the artificial housing crisis.

The WoonActie starts at 13:00 in the Poptapark. After the podium program with various speakers, we all go through the Delft streets to the Market. Anyone who supports us and the movement is welcome to join.

COVID MEASURES

We are still in a pandemic. Therefore urgent advice:

- Stay at home in case of symptoms
- Always keep 1,5 meters away
- Wear a face mask
- More accessibility information to follow!

The volunteers of atmosphere management also hand out mouth caps.

Can't attend the WoonAction? Don't worry! Our protest can be followed via the livestream on YouTube, the link of which will follow soon.
